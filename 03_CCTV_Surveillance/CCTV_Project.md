# 📹 CCTV Surveillance System – Warehouse / Industrial Facility

## 📍 Project Overview
Deployment and commissioning of an IP-based CCTV surveillance system for a warehouse/industrial facility to ensure 24/7 monitoring, asset protection, and operational security.

The system was designed to provide complete coverage of critical zones including entry/exit points, storage areas, and operational zones.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Ensure real-time monitoring of facility operations  
- Enhance security and theft prevention  
- Provide recorded video evidence for incident analysis  
- Enable centralized surveillance and remote access  

---

## 🔧 Scope of Work
- Installation of IP cameras (indoor & outdoor)  
- Structured cabling and termination  
- Network configuration (IP addressing, connectivity)  
- NVR installation and configuration  
- Camera alignment and focus adjustment  
- System testing and commissioning

## 🚧 Challenges & Solutions

### 🔸 Challenge 1: Network Connectivity Issues  
**Issue:** Intermittent video loss due to unstable network connection  
**Solution:**  
- Verified IP configuration and subnet settings  
- Checked switch connectivity and cable terminations  
- Ensured stable network communication  

---

### 🔸 Challenge 2: Poor Camera Coverage  
**Issue:** Blind spots in certain warehouse areas  
**Solution:**  
- Repositioned cameras strategically  
- Adjusted lens angles and focal length  
- Ensured complete coverage of critical zones  

---

### 🔸 Challenge 3: Storage Optimization  
**Issue:** Limited storage capacity for continuous recording  
**Solution:**  
- Optimized recording settings (frame rate & resolution)  
- Configured motion-based recording where required  
- Improved storage utilization
